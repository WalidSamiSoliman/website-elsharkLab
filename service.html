Services

Reliable & High-Quality Laboratory Service

Your health deserves the highest standards. Our laboratory offers dependable services with modern equipment and experienced experts, guaranteeing accurate results you can always trust.

Pathology Testing

Our pathology services provide precise and timely insights, helping doctors and patients make informed decisions for better health.

Microbiology Tests

Accurate microbiology tests to identify bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites, ensuring reliable diagnosis and effective treatment.

Biochemistry Tests

Our biochemistry services provide precise analysis of blood and body fluids, helping doctors monitor health conditions and guide effective treatments.

Pregnancy tests

Our pregnancy tests deliver quick and precise results, giving you confidence and clarity at every stage.

Urine Tests

Comprehensive urine analysis to help detect infections, monitor kidney function, and assess overall health.

Blood Tests

Comprehensive blood testing to assess overall health, detect diseases, and monitor ongoing treatments with accuracy and reliability.

Fever Tests

Our fever tests quickly detect common causes of fever, including bacterial and viral infections, ensuring fast and reliable results.

Allergy Tests

Comprehensive allergy testing to identify triggers such as food, dust, and pollen, helping manage allergic reactions effectively.
